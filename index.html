Navigation Solutions for Every Mission

Three powerful AI products, one seamless integration

VisualGPS

GPS-quality positioning from camera imagery alone. Navigate confidently in GPS-denied environments—urban canyons, under bridges, or during signal jamming—with centimeter-level accuracy.

  • Real-time visual positioning
  • Works in GPS-denied environments
  • Centimeter-level accuracy
  • MAVLink and OSDK compatible output

LandingZone

Intelligent landing site detection using advanced computer vision. Automatically identifies safe, flat surfaces and guides your drone to a precision touchdown—even in uncharted terrain.

  • AI-powered surface analysis
  • Obstacle detection & avoidance
  • Terrain classification
  • Autonomous precision landing

Bando

Navigate the tightest spaces with confidence. Purpose-built for indoor environments, warehouses, tunnels, and confined areas where GPS doesn't reach and precision is critical.

  • Indoor SLAM navigation
  • Tight-space maneuvering
  • 3D environment mapping
  • Collision-free path planning
